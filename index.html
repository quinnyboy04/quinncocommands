Quinn&Co - Commands!

Fun:

q/8ball
Makes the magic 8-ball respond to your question.

q/bird
Sends you a random image of a bird, as well as a random bird fact!

q/cat

q/doggo

q/koala

Utility:

q/about <@user>
Gives you some information on another person!

q/aboutme
Gives you some information about yourself!

q/avatar (<@user>)
Gives you someone's avatar. If there is no ping, it just grabs your own avatar.

q/apiabuser
Gives you access to the API Abusers channel for programmers.
 
 

Moderation:

Only Owners, Admins and Mods can execute these commands!
 
q/ban <@user>
Bans a person from the guild.
 
q/kick <@user>
Kicks a person from the guild.
 
q/txtmute <@user>
Mutes a person in all text channels, so they cannot chat.
 
q/txtunmute <@user>
Unmutes a person in all text channels.
 
q/vcmute <@user>
Mutes a person in all VCs, so they cannot talk.
 
q/vcunmute <@user>
Unmutes a person in all VCs.
